3D Printing

FDM 3D Printing

Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) is the workhorse of 3D printing — great for functional parts, structural prototypes, enclosures, jigs, and anything that needs to be strong and cost-effective. We use a next-generation enclosed, heated-chamber machine built for speed, precision, and reliability.

  • Layer resolution down to 0.1 mm
  • Build volume up to 350 × 320 × 325 mm
  • Multi-color and multi-material capable
  • PLA, ABS, PETG, ASA, TPU, Nylon, and more
  • Post-processing: sanding, priming, painting available
  • Tolerances to ±0.2 mm on typical geometries

Add-On Service
