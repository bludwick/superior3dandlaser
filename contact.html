FAQ

Common Questions

What file formats do you accept?

For 3D printing: STL, OBJ, 3MF, STEP. For laser work: SVG, DXF, AI, PDF, CorelDRAW CDR. Images (PNG, JPG) are accepted for engraving. We can also help convert or clean up files.

How long will my order take?

Standard turnaround is 3–5 business days from order approval. Rush orders (24-hour) are available for an additional fee, subject to current capacity. We'll confirm timing on your quote.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit/debit cards and PayPal. Payment is due upon order confirmation. For larger orders, we may request a deposit upfront.

Can you do large production runs?

Yes — we handle small to medium production quantities. For large volumes, reach out and we'll discuss lead times and volume pricing that makes sense for your project.

Do you ship orders?

Yes — we ship anywhere in the US via USPS, UPS, or FedEx. Shipping cost is calculated at checkout. Local pickup is also available at no charge.

What if I don't have a design file?

No worries! Describe your project and we can help with design. We offer CAD modeling and artwork services to get your idea into a print-ready file.
